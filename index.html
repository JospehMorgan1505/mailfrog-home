Our Story

Built by B2B veterans.
Tired of broken tools.

Mailfrog was born inside Avention Media — a data-driven B2B marketing company with over a decade of experience helping enterprise brands reach the right buyers, at the right time.

After years of running outbound campaigns for clients, the Avention Media team kept running into the same wall: great data trapped in one tool, great execution locked in another — with an invoice for each. So we built the platform we always wished existed.

Mailfrog is Avention Media's dedicated outbound engine — combining proprietary data infrastructure with campaign execution in one unified product. The B2B expertise that powered hundreds of client campaigns is now available to every growth team.
